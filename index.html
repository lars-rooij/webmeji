Webmeji ৻( •̀ ᗜ •́ ৻)

Hi! the beloved desktop pet shimeji can now, instead of being only visible on the client side, be shown to everyone that visits your website! This version currently can do the following actions randomly:

  • Walk around on the bottom of your page!
  • Sit down for a random period!
  • Do a little silly dance!
  • Turn around!
  • And trip over itself!
Unlike the original shimeji, webmeji cannot (yet o.o): multiply, jump, climb, or get interacted with by the mouse.

You can: give a different appearence to the shimeji by replacing its frames, or add whole new animations and whatnot youself by changing the code!

Play around with the frequency of actions, frames, speeds, whatever! make your website a bit more fun by giving it its own webmeji ^-^

Get the files to put webmeji on YOUR website HERE, and find frames of your special character online to make them walk around for EVERYONE to see!!!
